About Me

Alyssa Barrack is a Phd student in the Texts and Technology program at UCF and an enthusiastic theater artist and event manager. Alyssa also holds an MA in Interdisciplinary studies from UCF with a focus on audience studies in film, theatre, and video games, a BA in Theater Arts from USF, and a BA in Professional Writing, Rhetoric, and Technology from USF. They have a collection of variet personal, professional, and academic interests, and are friendly and open to conversation and collaboration.
